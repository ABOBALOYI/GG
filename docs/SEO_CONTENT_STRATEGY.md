# Grant Guide SA: SEO Content Strategy & Copywriting Guidelines

## 1. Role & Objective
**Role:** SEO Content Strategist and Copywriter  
**Objective:** Create high-ranking, helpful, trustworthy SEO content to help South African businesses find funding.

**Target Audience:**
- South African SMEs
- Startups
- Informal/township businesses
- Youth and women entrepreneurs
- Established companies looking for expansion

**Core Principles:**
- Simple, direct, practical English
- South Africa-focused context
- Structured for SEO & featured snippets
- Accurate & non-misleading (Never promise funding approval)

---

## 2. Content Goals
1. **Rank on Google** for SA business funding searches.
2. **Help users** find opportunities faster.
3. **Encourage engagement** (search site, subscribe to alerts).
4. **Build trust** with clear steps, official links, and requirements.

---

## 3. SEO Keywords Strategy
Use natural variations of:
- Business grants South Africa
- SME funding South Africa
- Small business funding South Africa
- Government grants for small business South Africa
- Funding for startups South Africa
- Grants for youth-owned businesses South Africa
- Grants for women-owned businesses South Africa
- Funding for township businesses
- Business loans and grants South Africa
- How to apply for business funding South Africa

*Note: Include "South Africa" or "SA" naturally throughout.*

---

## 4. Required Content Structure (Template)

### SEO Metadata
- **SEO Title:** Max 60 chars
- **Meta Description:** Max 155 chars
- **URL Slug:** Clean, keyword-rich
- **Primary Keyword:** Defined
- **Secondary Keywords:** 5-10
- **Search Intent:** Informational / Commercial
- **Internal Links:** "Browse grants by [category]"
- **CTA:** Email alerts / Browse listings

### Article Body
1. **H1:** Must include primary keyword.
2. **Intro:** 2-3 sentences, direct SA context, defines who it's for.
3. **H2: Quick Answer:** Featured snippet style, 3-6 bullet points, no fluff.
4. **H2: Who qualifies?** Requirements (CIPC, Tax, B-BBEE mention where relevant).
5. **H2: Grants vs Loans vs Equity:** Simple comparison.
6. **H2: Where to find [topic] funding in SA:** List funders (Gov, DFI, Private).
7. **H2: Documents you normally need:** Bullets (CIPC, Bank statements, Tax clearance, etc).
8. **H2: Step-by-step application:** Numbered steps 1-7.
9. **H2: Common mistakes:** Real issues (missing docs, deadlines).
10. **H2: FAQs:** Min 6 South African questions.
11. **Final Section:** Next steps (Search Grant Guide SA, alerts, official links).

---

## 5. Content Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- ❌ Do NOT promise funding approval.
- ❌ Do NOT publish scammy content.
- ❌ Do NOT invent funders/programmes.
- ✅ Always recommend official links.
- ✅ Encourage verification on funder site.
- ✅ Tone: Helpful, professional, no emojis in body text.

---

## 6. Schema & Extras
**Schema Block:**
- Article summary (2 lines)
- Top 5 takeaways
- FAQ Schema (Question + Answer pairs)

**Style:**
- Short paragraphs.
- Simple English.
- Mobile-friendly.
